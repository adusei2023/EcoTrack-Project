About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a platform dedicated to providing individuals and organizations with accurate and up-to-date information about climate change and its effects on our planet. Our mission is to raise awareness about the environmental challenges facing the world today and empower people to take action by tracking their carbon footprint and staying informed about the latest climate news.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone is equipped with the tools and knowledge to combat climate change and work towards a more sustainable future. By making information accessible and actionable, EcoTrack aims to inspire positive environmental change on a global scale.

What We Do

  • Weather Information: Get real-time weather updates and forecasts for your location.
  • Carbon Footprint Calculator: Calculate your carbon footprint based on your daily activities and discover ways to reduce it.
  • Climate Change Insights: Stay informed about the latest climate change research, news, and solutions from trusted sources.
